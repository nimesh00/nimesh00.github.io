About Me

I am a PMRF (Prime Minister's Research Fellowship) fellow pursuing my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at IIT Kanpur, under the supervision of Prof. Shakti S. Gupta and Prof. Mangal Kothari. My research focuses on learning-based control systems for legged robots.

I am also the Co-Founder and CTO (Software) at xTerra Robotics, where my team and I have developed SVAN M2, a commercial quadruped robot designed for industrial inspection, hazardous environment navigation, and security patrols.

My work focuses on software development and motion control research for legged robots, particularly for industrial inspection and surveillance applications. I design and implement control systems for autonomous mobile robots and test them through physics-based simulations before deploying on real hardware.
