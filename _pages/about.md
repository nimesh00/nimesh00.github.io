Hello! I’m Nimesh Khandelwal, a passionate researcher and engineer currently pursuing my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at IIT Kanpur. My academic journey began in Jaipur, Rajasthan, where I completed my schooling before heading to the Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT) Jaipur for my B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering.

During my time at MNIT, I immersed myself in the world of robotics as an active member of [ZINE](https://zine.co.in), the institute's research and robotics group. This experience was foundational, pushing me to participate in numerous robotics competitions across prestigious institutions like IIT Kharagpur and IIT Bombay. These hands-on challenges not only honed my practical skills but also solidified my career path in robotics.

My core drive lies in tackling complex, real-world problems. I specialize in designing robust yet elegant control systems for autonomous mobile robots, rigorously testing these systems through high-fidelity physics-based simulations before deploying them on physical platforms. This iterative process of design, simulation, and real-world validation is at the heart of my approach.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I find balance in reading, playing video games, and engaging in sports. These activities provide a different kind of challenge and relaxation, fueling my curiosity and creativity.

I am always eager to connect with fellow enthusiasts, researchers, and professionals. Feel free to explore my work, and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to discuss ideas, research, or opportunities in robotics and mechanical engineering.
