My intellectual curiosity spans a wide range of topics within robotics and mechanical engineering, complemented by a few personal pursuits that keep me balanced and inspired.

### Research Interests

My primary research interests revolve around solving problems related to **non-linear dynamical systems**, with a strong focus on their stability analysis and control. I am particularly fascinated by:

*   **Estimation:** Developing robust algorithms for state estimation in complex robotic systems.
*   **Motion Planning:** Crafting efficient and safe motion plans for autonomous agents in dynamic environments.
*   **Low-Level Controller Development:** Designing precise and responsive controllers for robotic actuators.
*   **Autopilot Systems:** Exploring the development of autonomous navigation and control systems for various robotic platforms.

Currently, my research is concentrated on the **dynamics, control, and stability analysis of legged robots**, with a specific emphasis on **quadruped robots**. This involves understanding their complex gaits, balance, and interaction with challenging terrains.

Outside of my research, I enjoy:

*   **Reading:** Delving into books across various genres, from technical literature to fiction, to broaden my perspectives.
*   **Video Games:** Engaging with interactive narratives and strategic challenges in the digital world.
*   **Sports:** Participating in physical activities to stay active and clear my mind.
